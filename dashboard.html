Get Your Questions Answered!

We have answered below the most common questions about our flow feature.

What is a Flow?

The Flows feature is a robust tool designed to help users manage internal projects such as social media, sales, marketing, and more. It provides a centralized platform where teams can collaborate, communicate, assign tasks, write notes, and effortlessly track progress within each project.

How does the Flows feature benefit me as a user?

The Flows feature offers numerous benefits, including streamlined project management, enhanced team collaboration, improved communication, efficient task assignment, comprehensive progress tracking, and centralized documentation of project-related information.

What can I do within a flow?

Once you have created a flow/project, you can perform various actions within it. You can initiate real-time chat conversations with your team members, assign tasks to individuals or groups, write and save project-related notes, and easily monitor the progress of tasks and milestones.

Can I customize each flow?

Absolutely! Each flow is fully customizable to suit your specific needs. You can tailor the flow settings, add relevant details such as project description or objectives, upload supporting files, and organize the flow structure in a way that aligns with your workflow.

Can I share files and documents through the chat feature inside a flow?

Absolutely! Our software allows you to securely share files and documents directly within the chat, streamlining collaboration and communication.

Can I communicate with my team within the Flows feature?

Absolutely! The Flows feature includes a built-in chat functionality that enables seamless communication with your team members. You can have real-time discussions, share updates, ask questions, and collaborate effectively within the flow environment.

Is the Flows feature available on mobile devices?

Yes, the Flows feature is accessible on both web and mobile platforms. You can conveniently manage your flows/projects, communicate with your team, assign tasks, and track progress using our mobile app, ensuring productivity on the go.
