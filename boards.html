We have answered below the most common questions about the CRM

What is a CRM tool?

A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) tool is a software application that helps businesses manage and analyze customer interactions, track sales activities, and nurture relationships.

How does your CRM tool work?

Our CRM tool utilizes a board-based system, similar to Trello, to organize and track customer-related activities. Boards represent customers, cards represent interactions or tasks, and lists categorize stages or statuses.

Can I customize the CRM boards and cards?

Yes, our CRM tool allows customization. You can create custom fields, add labels, and define stages to align with your unique business processes and track customer interactions in a way that suits your needs.

Can I collaborate with my team using the CRM tool?

Absolutely! Our CRM tool supports collaboration by enabling you to invite team members, assign tasks, leave comments, and track progress together. This promotes efficient teamwork and ensures a unified approach to customer management.

Is the CRM tool mobile-friendly?

Yes, our CRM tool is designed with mobile responsiveness in mind. You can access and manage your CRM boards and cards on both iOS and Android devices using our mobile applications.

Can I integrate the CRM tool with other systems or applications?

Yes, our CRM tool offers integrations with popular tools such as email clients, calendars, project management platforms, and more. These integrations streamline workflows, enhance productivity, and ensure data consistency. (In progress)

How secure is the CRM tool?

We prioritize data security and employ robust measures to protect your customer data. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular data backups. Rest assured, your information is safe with us.

Is customer support available for the CRM tool?

Yes, we provide customer support to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter while using our CRM tool. Reach out to our support team via email, live chat, or community forums.
