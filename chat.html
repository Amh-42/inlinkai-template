Get Your Questions Answered!

We have answered below the most common questions about our chat feature.

Is there a limit on the number of messages I can send?

No there is no limit to the amount of messages you, your team and clients can send on the platform.

How secure is the chat, audio, and video communication?

Our platform employs end-to-end encryption to ensure the security and privacy of all communications, including chat, audio, and video calls.

Can I create group chats and conference calls?

Yes, our software supports both group chats and conference calls, allowing you to collaborate effectively with multiple participants simultaneously.

Is there a limit on the number of participants in a video call or chat?

The software supports a generous number of participants; however, the specific limit may vary depending on the subscription plan you choose. With the Solo plan you can only chat with clients, for the standard plan you can add up to 3 of your team members and with the premium plan you can add up to 100 people in the same call.

Can I share files and documents through the chat feature?

Absolutely! Our software allows you to securely share files and documents directly within the chat, streamlining collaboration and communication.

Can I record audio and video calls for future reference?

Yes, our platform offers call recording functionality, enabling you to capture audio and video calls for future review or sharing with team members.

How do I invite external participants to join a call or chat?

You can easily invite external participants by sharing a unique meeting link, which will grant them access to join the call or chat without requiring an account on the platform.

Is customer support available for the chat?

Yes, we provide customer support to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter while using our chat. Reach out to our support team via email, live chat, or community forums.
