Get Your Questions Answered!

We have answered below the most common questions about the invoice feature.

Which payment methods do offer?

We currently integrate with Stripe which allows clients to pay using the following: all major credit and debit cards, Apple pay and Google pay, bank transfer using ACH credit transfers in the US and SEPA and BACS in the EU. We are also working on integrating with PayPal.

What is an invoicing software?

Invoicing software is a digital tool designed to automate and simplify the process of creating and sending invoices to clients. It helps businesses generate professional invoices, track payments, and manage their billing efficiently.

How does invoicing software benefit my business?

Invoicing software offers numerous benefits for your business. It saves time by automating invoice creation and sending. It improves cash flow by enabling faster payments and reducing late payments. It also provides organization and transparency in tracking invoices, payments, and financial data.

Can I customize my invoices?

Yes, our invoicing software allows you to customize your invoices. You can add your company logo, personalized messages, and choose from various invoice templates. This customization helps maintain your brand identity and professionalism.

Can I invoice in multiple currencies?

Absolutely! Our invoicing software supports invoicing in multiple currencies. You can easily create invoices in different currencies, enabling you to cater to international clients and simplify currency conversions.

Is it possible to track invoice payments?

Yes, our software provides tools to track invoice payments. You can monitor the status of each invoice, view payment due dates, and send automated reminders for outstanding payments. This feature helps you stay on top of your finances and ensure timely payments.

Can I integrate the software with my accounting system?

We offer integrations with popular accounting systems, making it seamless to sync your invoicing data with your accounting software. This integration eliminates manual data entry and ensures accurate financial records.

Is my data secure with your invoicing software?

Yes, we prioritize data security. Our software implements robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of your sensitive information.

Can I access my invoices and data from anywhere?

Absolutely! Our invoicing software is cloud-based, allowing you to access your invoices and data from anywhere with an internet connection. Whether you're in the office or on the go, you can manage your invoicing tasks conveniently.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial period for you to explore and experience the features of our invoicing software. It's a risk-free way to evaluate its suitability for your business before making a commitment.

What customer support options are available?

We provide customer support through various channels, including email, live chat, and phone. Our dedicated support team is ready to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter while using our invoicing software.
