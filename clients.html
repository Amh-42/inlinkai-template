Get Your Questions Answered!

We have answered below the most common questions about our client feature.

What is the Clients feature?

The Clients feature is a powerful tool that allows users to create custom workspaces dedicated to their clients. Within these workspaces, users can engage in seamless communication, assign tasks, write notes, and effortlessly track all ongoing work for each client.

How does the Clients feature benefit me as a user?

The Clients feature offers numerous benefits, including the ability to centralize client-related activities in one organized workspace. It facilitates efficient collaboration, keeps you on top of tasks, promotes clear communication, and provides a comprehensive overview of all the work being done for each client.

How do I create a client space?

Creating a client workspace is simple. Within the platform, navigate to the Clients section, and click on the "Create New Client" button. From there, you can enter the client's name and set up the space according to your preferences.

What can I do within a client workspace?

Once you have created a client workspace, you can perform various actions. You can engage in real-time chat conversations with your clients, assign tasks to team members or yourself, write and save notes related to the client's project, and keep track of the progress being made.

Can I customize each client workspace?

Absolutely! Each client workspace is fully customizable to cater to your specific needs. You can personalize the workspace by adding relevant project details, uploading relevant files, and organizing the workspace in a way that suits your workflo

Can clients access the workspace?

Yes, you have the option to invite clients to access the workspace. You can provide them with secure login credentials, allowing them to participate in discussions, view assigned tasks, and stay updated on the progress of their project.

Is the Clients feature available on mobile devices?

Yes, the Clients feature is accessible on both web and mobile platforms. You can conveniently manage client workspaces, communicate with clients, assign tasks, and track progress on the go using our mobile app.

How secure is the Clients feature?

We prioritize the security and privacy of your data. Our platform employs robust security measures to safeguard your client workspaces, including encryption, secure access controls, and regular data backups. You can trust that your client's information and project details are protected.
